CONSULTATION-TO-CLIENT SNIPPET PACK
For use with Typedesk (or any text expansion tool)
James | The Automated Solo | theautomatedsolo.com

HOW TO USE THIS FILE
--------------------
Each snippet below is a ready-to-use email template.
In Typedesk, create a new template for each one, assign
a short trigger (like :consult-confirm or :retainer-send),
and use Typedesk's variable fields to fill in the bracketed
placeholders at send time.

Variables you will set inside Typedesk for each snippet:
  {{client_name}}     - Client's first name
  {{date}}            - Consultation date (e.g., April 10, 2026)
  {{time}}            - Consultation time with timezone (e.g., 2:00 PM ET)
  {{meeting_link}}    - Your Zoom or Google Meet URL
  {{fee}}             - Consultation fee you charged (e.g., $150)
  {{practice_area}}   - Your practice area (e.g., family law, estate planning)
  {{your_name}}       - Your name
  {{firm_name}}       - Your firm name
  {{your_email}}      - Your direct email address
  {{retainer_amount}} - Retainer fee (e.g., $2,500)
  {{matter_type}}     - Type of matter (e.g., divorce, will and trust package)

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SNIPPET 1: CONSULTATION CONFIRMATION
Trigger suggestion: :consult-confirm
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Subject: Your Consultation is Confirmed for {{date}}

Hi {{client_name}},

You're booked. Here's what to expect before we talk.

Your consultation is scheduled for {{date}} at {{time}}.
Join here: {{meeting_link}}

To make the most of our 30 minutes, please have these
three things ready:

1. A one-paragraph summary of your situation. You don't
   need to write an essay. Just the basic facts and what
   outcome you're hoping for.

2. Any relevant documents. If you have a court order,
   a contract, or a prior attorney's letter related to
   your issue, upload it or have it open on your screen.
   I can review it faster than you can describe it.

3. Your questions. Write them down before we start.
   Consultations move fast. The clients who get the most
   value come with a list.

The consultation fee of {{fee}} was charged at booking.
That covers 30 minutes of direct legal analysis, not a
general Q&A session.

See you on {{date}}.

{{your_name}}
{{firm_name}}
{{your_email}}

SNIPPET 2: POST-CONSULTATION FOLLOW-UP (SAME DAY)
Trigger suggestion: :consult-followup
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Subject: Next Steps After Our Call Today

Hi {{client_name}},

Thanks for talking with me earlier today.

Based on what you described, I think I can help you with
your {{matter_type}} matter. Here is what happens next
if you want to move forward.

I'm going to send you an engagement letter and a fee
agreement within the next few hours. It will come from
BunnyDoc, an eSign platform I use instead of DocuSign.
You do not need to create an account. You open the email,
read the document, and click to sign. It takes about
two minutes.

The retainer amount is {{retainer_amount}}. Instructions
for payment are included in the letter.

If you have questions before you sign, reply to this
email. I typically respond within a few hours on business
days.

If I haven't heard back from you in 48 hours, I'll follow
up once. After that, I'll assume you've decided to go a
different direction, and I'll release the time I've held
for your matter.

{{your_name}}
{{firm_name}}
{{your_email}}

here and I'll send a corrected version.

I appreciate you trusting me with this.

NOTES ON TONE
---------------------------------------------------------------------
These templates are intentionally direct. Solo attorneys
often over-soften follow-up emails out of fear of seeming
pushy. That's a mistake. Clients who are serious about
moving forward appreciate clear, professional communication.
Clients who are not serious won't be offended by directness.

Every template here uses plain text formatting by design.
Do not add HTML styling, your firm logo, or social media
icons. Plain text emails have significantly higher open
and reply rates with legal consumers compared to
visually designed newsletter-style emails.

Import these into Typedesk, assign your triggers, and
you will spend less than two minutes on client
communications per new matter from first booking to
signed retainer.
