POST-CONSULTATION FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE
The Automated Solo | theautomatedsolo.com
=========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
This file contains the exact email copy for a three-part follow-up sequence that
runs automatically after every consultation you complete through TidyCal. Set it
up once inside SendFox, hook it to Pabbly Connect, and it runs forever.

To use this: paste each email into SendFox as a separate step in an automation.
Set Email 1 to send immediately (or same day), Email 2 at 3 days, Email 3 at 10 days.

In Pabbly Connect, create a workflow that triggers on new TidyCal bookings and adds
the contact to your SendFox "Consultation Leads" list with the tag "post-consult".
The automation fires automatically from there.

Replace ALL placeholders throughout the emails before loading into SendFox:
  {FIRST_NAME}      - contact's first name (pulled automatically by SendFox merge tag)
  {PRACTICE_AREA}   - your specific area (e.g., "family law," "immigration," "estate planning")
  {YOUR_NAME}       - your name as you want clients to see it
  [YOUR EMAIL]      - your direct email address
  [PHONE NUMBER]    - your direct phone number
  [BOOKING LINK]    - your TidyCal booking link

Also review the "getting your paperwork in order" language in Email 1 and replace it
with the actual next step that applies to your practice area and case type.
For example: "the next step is scheduling a document review," or "the next step is
signing a fee agreement so I can open your file."

=========================================

EMAIL 1: Same Day (Send Within 2 Hours of Consultation)
---------------------------------------------------------
Subject: Quick note after our call today

Hi {FIRST_NAME},

Thanks for taking the time to talk today. I wanted to send a short note
before the day gets away from me.

Based on what you told me, here is where things stand: you have a real
path forward. We covered the basics today, but if you want to move ahead,
the next step is getting your paperwork in order so I can give you a firm
answer on timeline and cost.

You can reach me directly at [YOUR EMAIL]. No contact form, no intake staff.
Just me.

If you decide to move forward, reply here or book a follow-up at [BOOKING LINK].

{YOUR_NAME}
[PHONE NUMBER]


---------------------------------------------------------

EMAIL 2: 3 Days After Consultation
-----------------------------------
Subject: Still thinking it over?

Hi {FIRST_NAME},

I know {PRACTICE_AREA} issues can feel overwhelming. Most people spend a week
paralyzed before doing anything, and then the situation gets harder to solve.

I am not going to pressure you. But I will tell you that the questions you
asked me are the same ones I hear from clients who end up in much better
shape six months later, specifically because they moved sooner rather than later.

If you have questions you forgot to ask, reply to this email. No charge for
a five-minute clarification.

{YOUR_NAME}


---------------------------------------------------------

EMAIL 3: 10 Days After Consultation
--------------------------------------
Subject: Last check-in from me

Hi {FIRST_NAME},

This is the last time I will reach out. I do not want to flood your inbox.

If your situation has changed, or if you simply found a different path
forward, I genuinely wish you luck. If you are still looking for someone
to handle this, I am still available and my calendar is at [BOOKING LINK].

One thing I hear from clients who waited: they wish they had started earlier.
That is not a sales pitch. It is just pattern recognition from seeing a lot
of these cases.

Either way, take care of yourself.

{YOUR_NAME}
[PHONE NUMBER]


=========================================

PABBLY CONNECT SETUP NOTES
---------------------------
Trigger: TidyCal > New Booking
Action 1: SendFox > Add/Update Contact (map Name and Email from TidyCal payload)
Action 2: SendFox > Add Tag to Contact (tag value: "post-consult")
Automation in SendFox: set list trigger to "when tag added" = "post-consult"

Field mapping in Pabbly Connect:
  - SendFox "First Name"  <- TidyCal booking "invitee_first_name"
  - SendFox "Email"       <- TidyCal booking "invitee_email"
  - SendFox "List ID"     <- your "Consultation Leads" list ID

You can find the TidyCal webhook URL under: Settings > Integrations > Webhooks.
Paste it into the Pabbly Connect TidyCal trigger module when setting up the workflow.
