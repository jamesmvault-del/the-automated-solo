## The Workflow: Consolidating Your Digital Footprint

You have a LinkedIn profile, a primary law firm website, a booking link for consultations, and likely a few published articles. When a prospective client finds you online, sending them to a generic homepage creates massive friction. You lose them to a messy navigation bar.

SleekBio generates a clean, mobile-optimized landing page designed to house all of your high-value links in one place. I deploy it as the absolute sole link in my social media bios to funnel traffic exactly where I need it to go.

1. **The Build:** I dropped in my firm logo, added a stark two-sentence bio, and linked heavily to my TidyCal booking page. It took me seven minutes.
2. **The Embeds:** You can directly embed YouTube videos or podcast episodes right onto the page. Clients can watch you speak without bouncing over to social media and getting distracted by another creator.
3. **The Analytics:** It tracks exactly which links are getting clicked so you actually know where your consultation bookings originate from.
